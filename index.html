About

Lilac Zhou was born in the city of Changchun in North East China. From 2012 to 2022, She studied Materials Engineering (Bridging course, Bachelor’s degree and PhD degree) in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, majored in 3D printing and shape memory ceramics, which provides her a deep understanding in materials. During the ten years living in the tropic climate, she always dreams about the beautiful scene and people in her hometown and she would like to turn this emotion into her artworks to evoke a sense of missing, seeking, and a quiet intimacy with nature.


Lilac's ceramic studio will be based in Suzhou, China, a city closed to Shanghai. The Suzhou City has consistent layout through 2000 years, plenty of flowers all over the year, small rivers along each small street, and traditional poems and music floating on each river. She keeps a lot of plants and two rabbits with her husband in Suzhou.


She is also interested in poems, Chinese calligraphy & seal carving, drawing, music, and any kinds of art.

Research interest: Ceramic art, glass art, 3D printing, sculpture

Artwork - Thoughts in the wind

Touch the nano-world - Morning

Medium: Stoneware with glaze
? cm * ? cm * ? cm
2021
Nano world is always felt by the scanning electron microscope, which does not provide color, sound, or tactile sensations. I want people to get other feelings about the nano-world. In this work, I took 5 common objects we use in the morning and use the scanning electron microscope to take the photo at high magnifications: White: tissue paper - 1000x; Black: rubber band - 20000x; Green: tea leave - 10000x; Top blue: towel - 10000x; Bottom blue: tooth brush bristles - 20000x.

Snow owl with Xu Wei's caligraphy

Medium: Stoneware with glaze
40 cm * 18.5 cm * 16 cm
2021
Inspired from the artist Miki, I made a cute snow owl. I think the white color is a good background for the Chinese caligraphy. Xu Wei (徐渭) was a Ming dynasty Chinese painter, poet, writer and dramatist famed for his artistic expressiveness. I love his caligraphy, so I wrote his caligraphy onto the snow owl. Both Xu Wei and the snow owl are nobel and sullied with a strong personality.
