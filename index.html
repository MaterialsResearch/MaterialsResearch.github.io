About

Lilac Zhou was born in the city of Changchun in North East China. From 2012 to 2022, She studied Materials Engineering (Bridging course, Bachelorâ€™s degree and PhD degree) in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, majored in 3D printing and shape memory ceramics, which provides her a deep understanding in materials. During the ten years living in the tropic climate, she always dreams about the beautiful scene and people in her hometown and she would like to turn this emotion into her artworks to evoke a sense of missing, seeking, and a quiet intimacy with nature.


Lilac's ceramic studio will be based in Suzhou, China, a city closed to Shanghai. The Suzhou City has consistent layout through 2000 years, plenty of flowers all over the year, small rivers along each small street, and traditional poems and music floating on each river. She keeps a lot of plants and two rabbits with her husband in Suzhou.


She is also interested in poems, Chinese calligraphy & seal carving, drawing, music, and any kinds of art.
